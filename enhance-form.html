Lab Safety Steps

  1. Wear appropriate PPE (lab coat, gloves, goggles).
  2. Disinfect your workspace thoroughly.
  3. Check equipment calibration and functionality.
  4. Label all samples clearly.
  5. Dispose of waste according to biohazard protocols.

Comparison Table: Equipment vs. Use Case

Equipment Primary Use Sample Type Notes
Microscope Cell observation Blood, tissue Requires staining for clarity
Centrifuge Separation of components Blood, urine Speed and duration affect results
Spectrophotometer Measuring absorbance Serum, plasma Needs proper calibration
Pipettes Liquid transfer Any liquid sample Use appropriate volume range
Incubator Culture growth Any biological culture Monitor temperature regularly
